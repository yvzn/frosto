Service terms of use

You remain the owner of the personal data you entrust to us. You can request their modification or deletion at any time. We strive to protect them as best as possible.

Personal data

The information collected on the registration form is recorded in a computerized file by FrostAlert.net to analyze weather forecasts and send alerts. The legal basis for processing is user consent.

The collected data may indirectly be communicated to the following recipients only: Tipimail (email service provider).

Data is kept for the duration of the user's subscription to the service and up to 7 days after the user has notified their unsubscription, for technical reasons (data cleanup).

You can access the data concerning you, rectify it, request its deletion or exercise your right to limit the processing of your data. You can withdraw your consent to the processing of your data at any time. You can also object to the processing of your data. You can also exercise your right to data portability.

Visit the GDPR website for more information about your rights.

To exercise these rights or for any question about the processing of your data in this system, you can contact us.

If you believe, after contacting us, that your Information and Freedoms rights are not respected, you can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.

How it works

This free service is maintained by volunteers.

Once registered, we analyze weather forecasts and send an alert when temperatures close to zero or negative are forecast in your city.

You can stop at any time.
