A free service

This frost alert email service is maintained by volunteers and remains entirely free for all users. It will always stay that way.

You have no obligation to make a donation to use the service. Whether you donate or not, you will receive exactly the same service.

Infrastructure costs

Even though the service is free, it generates operating costs: hosting of the website and application, sending alert emails, use of weather services.

These costs vary depending on the number of users and the volume of emails sent, around a few dozen euros per year.

Currently, these costs are personally supported by the service maintainers. A donation helps us cover these costs and ensure the sustainability of the service.

Every contribution, even modest, is valuable and allows us to continue offering this service to everyone.

How to donate

You can contribute any amount of your choice, via the PayPal platform.

We warmly thank you for your support!
