Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get frost alerts near me?

Sign up with your city and country. We'll send free frost alerts near you when forecasts predict temperatures close to or below 0°C.

Can I receive frost alerts for my area only?

Yes. Alerts are personalized to your area, so you only get notified when frost is expected around your location.

Will it help protect frost-sensitive outdoors appliances or plants?

Yes. A timely frost alert helps you take action to protect your outdoor appliances.

A frost warning sent in advance helps you cover plants or move pots before a cold snap.

Are frost alerts available for my city?

Yes. Frost Alerts are available for any city worldwide, thanks to our global weather data provider.

Is this a free frost alert app?

Yes, Frost Alerts is free. No other commercial solicitations from us.
